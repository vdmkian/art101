Pseudocode of common task (we picked making french toast):

  • get long flat container
  • crack 1 egg for each piece of toast you want
  • add 1 tablespoon of milk for each egg cracked
  • add ½ teaspoon of salt from each egg cracked
  • add ½ tablespoon of sugar for each egg cracked
  • add ½ tablespoon cinnamon for each egg cracked
  • whisk all ingredients together in the long flat container
  • turn on stove top to heat it up
  • grab slices of bread for amount of toast intended
  • soak toast in egg mixture until all egg mixture is soaked up
  • add butter to already heated pan
  • put soaked bread on hot pan
  • flip after 2 min
  • after 2 more min take off heat
  • put toast on a plate
  • add toppings and enjoy
